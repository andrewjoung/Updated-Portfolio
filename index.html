About Me

Throughout the years I have gained a lot of exposure to the world of software development and have experience in Java, Android Development, and front-end web development technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and relevant JavaScript libraries such as jQuery and React.js. By the end of summer 2019, it is my goal and dream to be an outstanding full-stack web developer.

Since a young age, I have always been drawn to good design and beautiful aesthetics and I aim to showcase both these elements in my projects. While I do not have much design experience, I strive to learn more about good design practices and principles with every project I do.
